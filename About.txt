Electron Wizard
A webapp to help in designing of electronics

Features:
- Simple interface 
- Runs on any device with a browser and python (3.6 or above)

Calculators for:
1) Ohms law
2) Through hole resistor value
3) SMD Resistor value
4) SMD Capacitor value
5) SMD Inductor value
6) Voltage divider
7) Resistors in series/parallel
8) LED resistor
